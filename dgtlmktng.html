Social Media Marketing

Social Media Marketing includes content marketing, where brands post valuable content; influencer marketing, where collaborations with influencers boost reach; and paid advertising, which uses targeted ads for promotions. It also involves community management to engage users and live streaming to interact in real time. Lastly, user-generated content helps build authenticity by sharing content from real customers.

Email Marketing

Email marketing includes promotional emails to showcase offers or products, transactional emails like order confirmations and receipts, and newsletter emails for regular updates or blog content. It also covers welcome emails for new subscribers, re-engagement emails for inactive users, and survey/feedback emails to gather customer insights. Each type serves a unique purpose in building customer relationships and driving engagement.

Pay-Per-Click Advertising

Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising is a digital marketing model where advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. It includes platforms like Google Ads, Bing Ads, and social media ads (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn). PPC helps drive targeted traffic quickly, with results measurable through impressions, clicks, and conversions.

Influencer Marketing

Influencer Marketing involves partnering with individuals who have a strong online presence and loyal followers to promote your brand, products, or services. These influencers can be celebrities, niche content creators, or micro-influencers, depending on the campaign goals. It helps build trust, expand reach, and boost engagement by leveraging the influencer's credibility.

Job Portals Handling

Handling all job portals involves efficiently managing multiple platforms like Naukri, LinkedIn, Indeed, and others to post jobs, track applications, and communicate with candidates. It includes creating attractive job listings, maintaining updated profiles, and actively sourcing the right talent. A centralized strategy or tool helps streamline recruitment across all portals for maximum reach and productivity.

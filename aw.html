Article Writting

Expository articles explain facts and concepts clearly without opinions, while descriptive articles create vivid imagery of people, places, or events. Persuasive articles aim to convince readers to adopt a viewpoint, and narrative articles tell engaging stories. Analytical articles break down complex topics for insights, and informative articles educate with factual, objective details.

Expository Articles

Explain facts, concepts, or ideas clearly and concisely.

Descriptive Articles

Focus on detailing an object, place, person, or event to create vivid imagery.

Persuasive Articles

Aim to convince readers to adopt a specific viewpoint or take action.

Narrative Articles

Tell a story, often personal, to engage readers emotionally.

Analytical Articles

Break down complex topics to analyze and interpret data or situations.

Informative Articles

Provide factual information on a topic to educate the audience.

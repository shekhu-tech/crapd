Video Editing

Video editing includes Linear Editing (L-cut & J-cut) for traditional cuts and Non-Linear Editing (NLE) with software like Premiere Pro for digital edits. Techniques like Montage Editing, Color Grading, and Cutaway Editing enhance storytelling and visuals. Motion Graphics & VFX add dynamic effects with animations and CGI.

Linear Editing

(L-cut & J-cut) Traditional method of editing video in a sequential manner using videotape.

Non-Linear Editing (NLE)

Digital editing using software like Adobe Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve, allowing flexible modifications.

Montage Editing

A series of short clips arranged together to show time progression or storytelling efficiently.

Color Grading & Correction

Adjusting colors, contrast, and brightness to create a specific mood or cinematic look.

Cutaway & B-roll Editing

Adding supplementary footage (B-roll) to complement the main footage (A-roll) for smooth transitions.

Motion Graphics & VFX Editing
